What are kulfis?


 Kulfis are similar to ice cream in appearance and taste, but it is denser and creamier. It also comes in various flavors with the major one being mango. Kulfis are known as the traditional Indian ice cream.​ Typically they are sold with either popsicle sticks or just on a plate. We serve on a popsicle stick without nuts and gluten.
