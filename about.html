Our Company

"Kulfi Kream was founded on the principle that every person should be able to eat desserts without any worry. Kulfi Kream makes desserts that people with nut and gluten allergies can consume. Traditional ice creams, the ones bought at local stores, usually have nuts or are made in a facility that has nuts in it. All products of Kulfi Kream contain no nuts and no nuts in their facilities."
Welcome to Kulfi Kream. We are passionate people who work hard to bring you delicious naturally sourced ingredients to make tasty kulfi for you and your family.

We deliver tasty kulfis from our family here at Kulfi Kream to your family. ​

Our Story

It all started in 3rd grade when I signed up to the international fair project. The point of the fair was to make something about your country of heritage. In my case, this was India. So the day came and I handed out the kulfis to the people who looked at my project. I asked for some feedback and everyone loved them so much! This gave me the passion and courage to start my business. I slowly started my business by selling my kulfis to my family and friends.  In the future we want to bring kulfis to all people even if they have nut allergies.
